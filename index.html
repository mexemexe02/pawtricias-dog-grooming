Meet Pawtricia

Caring Hands,
Happy Pets

With over 15 years of professional grooming experience, Pawtricia has worked with every breed — from tiny Chihuahuas to giant Newfoundlands — and yes, plenty of cats too. Her philosophy is simple: every pet deserves patience, kindness, and a stress-free experience.

She's certified in fear-free handling techniques and stays current with the latest grooming methods. Whether your pet is a grooming veteran or a nervous first-timer, they're in the safest hands in Barrie.

  • All breeds & cats welcome — no pet turned away
  • Fear-free certified — gentle, low-stress handling
  • Premium products — hypoallergenic & pet-safe
  • One-on-one attention — never rushed, always loved
