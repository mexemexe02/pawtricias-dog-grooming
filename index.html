Grooming for Every Breed — Feline Too!

From Great Danes to Persians, every pet gets individualized care tailored to their coat, temperament, and needs.

✂️

Full Groom

Bath, haircut, nail trim, ear cleaning — the complete package. Breed-specific cuts tailored to your preferences.

🛁

Bath & Brush

Shampoo, conditioner, blow-dry, and thorough de-shedding. Perfect maintenance between full grooms.

🐱

Cat Grooming

Yes, we groom cats! Gentle handling for lion cuts, sanitary trims, de-shedding, and nail caps. Stress-free for your feline friend.

💅

Nail Trim

Quick, gentle nail clipping and filing for dogs and cats. Walk-ins welcome when available.

🦷

Teeth Brushing

Fresh breath and healthy gums. Add to any grooming service.

Puppy & Kitten Intro

About Pawtricia

15 Years of Loving Grooms.

Pawtricia's Dog Grooming isn't a big-box salon — it's a home-based studio where your pet gets undivided, one-on-one attention. No cages, no chaos, no rush. Just a calm, quiet space where your fur baby feels safe.

With over 15 years of experience grooming all breeds AND cats, Pawtricia has seen it all — from anxious rescues to show-dog perfection. Every groom is customized. Every pet is treated like family.
